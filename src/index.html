Our Story

Established over 10 years ago, we're a small café/coffee shop located on the shore of beautiful San diego. The Royal Café is a classic and timeless café house which serves up a fresh home-roasted café to their guests. We know our guests’ time is valuable, and we want them to relax and enjoy our warm hospitality. The first sips of our coffee reveals its amazing fresh roasted taste and it's aroma relax your mind in no time.
